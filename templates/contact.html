Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you get started?

+

Most clients are onboarded and live within 10–14 business days. We move fast because we know time-to-pipeline matters. Our team handles all the setup so you can focus on your business.

What's the minimum engagement?

+

We typically work on 3-month minimum engagements to give campaigns enough runway to optimise. However, project-based work (like a single webinar) is also available with no minimum commitment.

Do you work with startups or only enterprise clients?

+

Both! We work with fast-growing B2B startups and established enterprise brands. Our programs are designed to scale with your growth — from a Series A startup targeting 50 accounts to a global enterprise running pan-India campaigns.

How do you measure success?

+

We agree on KPIs before we start — pipeline generated, cost-per-lead, qualified opportunities, and revenue influenced. You get a live dashboard with full visibility, plus monthly performance review calls.
