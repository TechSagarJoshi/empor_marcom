Our Story

Started in 2012.
Still Growing Strong.

Empor Marcom was founded in New Delhi with a single conviction: that B2B marketing in India needed to be smarter, faster, and more accountable. We started as a small team of passionate marketers obsessed with generating real business outcomes — not just impressions and clicks.

Today, we're a full-service B2B marketing agency with deep expertise in lead generation, webinars & events, content marketing, and performance campaigns. Our clients span technology, SaaS, manufacturing, financial services, and professional services.
